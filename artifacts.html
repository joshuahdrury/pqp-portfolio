Artifact 1: PLC Leadership – Math & Coding

Evidence
Led a Professional Learning Community to revise the mathematics curriculum to include coding. Supported staff through initial hesitation by modeling lessons, co-learning, and using a guided slideshow during PLC sessions.

OLF Alignment
Setting Directions · Building Relationships and Developing People · Leading the Instructional Program

Reflection
How did my leadership approach reduce staff anxiety around unfamiliar content? What strategies were most effective in building collective efficacy?

Impact
Teachers developed confidence in teaching coding and collaboratively embedded it into curriculum planning.
